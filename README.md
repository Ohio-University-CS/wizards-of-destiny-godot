Wizards of Destiny is a deckbuilding roguelike built using the Godot engine. 
Players progress through multiple levels, battling enemies using unique card decks and upgrading their abilities along the way. 
The game focuses on strategic decision-making, deck management, and progression through increasingly challenging stages.

Game Overview:
In Wizards of Destiny, you play as the powerful wizard Bill the Wise, and use magical cards to defeat enemies across a series of levels. 
Each level contains multiple stages that must be completed before moving forward. 
As players advance, they unlock new decks, discover stronger cards, and find powerful items to improve their chances of success.
The game encourages players to think strategically about which cards to play and when to upgrade them, creating a balance between planning ahead and adapting to different enemies.

Features:
9 Unique Decks: Each deck contains over 40 cards, offering a wide variety of abilities, spells, and strategies.
Progressive Level System: Players must unlock each level by completing the previous one. Each level includes multiple stages, increasing the challenge as the game progresses.
Card-Based Combat: Players use cards to attack, defend, and defeat enemies, requiring strategic decision-making to win battles.
Upgradeable Cards: After completing stages, players can upgrade cards to improve their effectiveness and customize their strategy.
Strategic Gameplay: Choosing the right deck and upgrading cards wisely is essential to progressing through the game.
